Turn regulars into
subscribers.

Retail stores — especially cannabis dispensaries — have loyal customers who visit weekly but no way to capture that loyalty as predictable, recurring revenue.

The Problem

  • Cannabis retailers can’t use standard loyalty or gift card systems due to banking restrictions
  • Credit card processing is expensive or unavailable for cannabis
  • No turnkey way to offer subscription memberships with custom perks
  • Revenue is unpredictable — entirely dependent on foot traffic

Teyra’s Answer

  • Launch a branded membership program with custom perks in minutes — no POS integration, no hardware
  • ACH subscription billing bank-to-bank — bypasses card network restrictions entirely
  • Members enroll in under 60 seconds via QR code → phone OTP → bank link
  • Predictable MRR from your most loyal customers
Teyra6

How It Works

1. Store Setup

Brand your portal with your logo, colors, and custom domain. Create membership tiers with pricing, discounts, and perks. Takes under 10 minutes.

2. Members Enroll

Customer scans a QR code, verifies their phone with OTP, links their bank via Plaid, and picks a tier. No app download. Under 60 seconds.

3. Revenue Flows

ACH payments auto-debit monthly via Dwolla. Staff verify members at checkout by phone number. Perks track and redeem automatically.

  • White-label branding — your logo, colors, fonts, and custom domain
  • Tiered memberships — unlimited tiers with custom pricing and perks
  • Staff validation — phone number lookup to verify membership at checkout
  • ACH payments — bank-to-bank, no card networks
  • Member portal — digital membership card, perk tracking, billing history
  • Real-time dashboard — MRR, churn, member growth, perk redemptions

BuddyBoost8
BuddyBoost

How It Works

1. Brands Fund

Create a campaign, set product rules (per-unit, per-dollar, tiered), choose target stores, and deposit budget. Full control over spend.

2. Stores Approve

Connect your POS (Dutchie). Review incoming campaigns. Approve the ones you want. Your staff start earning immediately.

3. Staff Earn

Sell qualifying products. BuddyBoost matches each sale to active campaigns via POS data. Rewards land in your wallet automatically.

  • Automated matching — POS transactions matched to campaign rules by SKU, category, or brand
  • Real-time wallets — staff see earnings as they happen
  • Brand analytics — see exactly which staff, stores, and products drive ROI
  • Invite system — unique codes link POS employees to BuddyBoost accounts
  • Flexible rules — per-unit, per-dollar, threshold, tiered, and category-based rewards
  • Instant or standard cashout — staff choose speed vs. fee
